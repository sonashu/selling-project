When we talk about wedding dresses for girls, the sky is the limit! Every other day we spot a new style, a new silhouette, and a completely new design. Which is why, there's no dearth of ideas and inspiration to brim our lovely brides with, ever. While on some days we're showcasing glamorous gowns and spell-binding silk sarees for all the fashion-savvy souls, there are other days when we're enlisting some of the prettiest suits, raging designer lehengas and trendy sharara & gharara sets for them. Not just that! We also keep putting forth varied wedding dresses for girls according to the colors that are trending for the season-like powder blues, yummy pinks, greens & lavenders. So we thought why not compile it all together and unleash some of our favorite designs to you? Yes, here's our elaborate compilation on some of the best wedding dresses for girls that we've come across in recent times! Scroll through and let the bookmarking begin!

Fresh & Latest Wedding Dresses for Girls

1. A classic red lehenga

What could be better than beginning our exclusive list of Indian wedding dresses for girls than with a typical traditional red lehenga?! Well, almost 7 out of 10 brides prefer picking a red lehenga for their big day even today. And for the fact that they look super bold yet elegant at the same time, even we find it hard to not fall for them each time we spot a new one on our brides. That's exactly what happened when we came across our real bride Nikita's wedding day look! She donned a deep red Anita Dongre lehenga with stunning meenakari & polki jewels on her big day and made for a beautiful bride.

Shot by Israni Photography, Mumbai
